The Setting

Mziki Private Game Reserve

Mziki Private Game Reserve is located in the heart of the internationally recognised Phinda Private Game Reserve, operated by &Beyond. The reserve spans over 240 hectares, with a traversing agreement across a further 2,300 hectares of pristine Zululand wilderness — big-five country, without the crowds.

It's a rare setup: the privacy and intimacy of a private homestead, with the ecology, game density and conservation credentials of one of South Africa's most celebrated reserves right outside the door.
