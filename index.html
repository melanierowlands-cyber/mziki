Views & Wildlife

A front-row seat on Fagolweni Dam

The property has stunning views over Fagolweni Dam — the largest body of water on the Munyawana / Phinda reserve, and the heart around which Mziki's 23 homes are arranged. From the deck, watch the sun rise behind the hills, game coming down to drink, or hippos hauling out for their midday nap.

As evening falls, you're often treated to the eerie call of a spotted hyena, a lion roaring in the distance, or the calls of the Fiery-necked Nightjar and elusive Wood Owl. For birders, the list is close to endless — Zululand is one of the most rewarding birding regions in the country.

The Setting

Mziki, at the heart of Munyawana / Phinda

Mziki Private Reserve sits at the centre of the award-winning, internationally renowned Munyawana / Phinda Private Reserve — 30,000 hectares in northern KwaZulu-Natal, bordering the 40,000-hectare Mkuze Game Reserve, now part of the iSimangaliso Wetland Park, a World Heritage Site covering more than 3,000 square kilometres.

The Mziki Share Block itself covers 240 hectares of land around the Fagolweni Dam and comprises 23 free-standing homes. As an integral part of this Big Five reserve, Mziki holds dominant, perpetual traversing rights across 2,600 hectares, where shareholders may self-drive in an open game-viewing vehicle. Access is exclusive to shareholders, their families, and guests they personally accompany.
